Official Standard Operating Procedures

1. Master Empanelment Agreement (MSA) & NDA ▼

This agreement outlines the legal relationship between Blue Wisdom and the Empanelled Trainer as an independent contractor.

  • Confidentiality (NDA): The Trainer agrees to maintain strict confidentiality regarding all Blue Wisdom intellectual property, training materials, client data, and commercial terms.
  • Intellectual Property: All decks, workbooks, and materials provided by Blue Wisdom remain the sole property of Blue Wisdom. The Trainer may not use, copy, or distribute these materials for external personal or professional assignments.
  • Non-Solicitation: The Trainer agrees not to directly solicit or accept parallel assignments from the specific Client assigned by Blue Wisdom for a period of 12 months post-deployment.
2. Code of Conduct & Facilitation Guidelines ▼

To ensure a standard of excellence, trainers representing Blue Wisdom must adhere to the following:

  • Brand Representation: The Trainer is representing Blue Wisdom. Do not distribute personal business cards or use personal company logos during the training.
  • Reporting Time: The Trainer must report to the training venue at least 45 minutes prior to the scheduled start time.
  • Energy & Engagement: Maintain consistently high energy and positive body language. Avoid sitting for extended periods; engage the audience interactively.
  • Content Adherence: Do NOT skip or alter the official PPT slides. Stick to the provided context, case studies, and activities unless specifically approved.
3. Travel, Stay & Expense (T&E) Policy ▼

For outstation deployments (e.g., Khavda, Bhuj), the following expense guidelines apply unless otherwise stated in your assignment brief:

  • Bookings: Travel and accommodation will either be pre-arranged by the Client/BW, or the Trainer will be provided a pre-approved budget cap for self-booking.
  • Reimbursements: All local conveyances (taxis) and meal expenses must be backed by original GST receipts. Hand-written bills without GST/PAN will not be reimbursed.
  • Submission: Expense claims must be submitted along with the final invoice within 48 hours of completing the deployment.
4. Commercials, Invoicing & Payouts ▼

Guidelines for professional fee processing:

  • Invoice Submission: Invoices must be submitted exclusively using the Official Blue Wisdom Invoice Template.
  • Mandatory Attachments: Invoices will NOT be processed without the submission of the physically signed participant attendance sheets.
  • TDS Deduction: Professional fees are subject to standard Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) as per government regulations.
  • Payment TAT: Approved invoices will be processed and disbursed within 30 to 45 days post-deployment, subject to client clearance.
