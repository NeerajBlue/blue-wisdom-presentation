Master Empanelment Agreement (MSA)

This Master Empanelment Agreement establishes the overarching terms of engagement between Blue Wisdom Pvt. Ltd. ("Company") and the Empanelled Facilitator/Trainer ("Independent Contractor").

1. Nature of Engagement

The Trainer is engaged exclusively as an Independent Contractor. This agreement does not create an employer-employee relationship, partnership, or joint venture. The Trainer is responsible for their own taxes, insurance, and statutory compliance.

2. Duration & Termination

This empanelment is valid for an initial period of one (1) year and auto-renews unless terminated. Either party may terminate this empanelment with a 30-day written notice. Blue Wisdom reserves the right to terminate immediately in cases of professional misconduct, breach of NDA, or client escalation.

3. Deployment & Acceptance

Blue Wisdom will notify the Trainer of potential deployments. The Trainer has the right to accept or decline. However, once accepted, the assignment is binding. Dropping an accepted assignment without a valid medical emergency may result in de-empanelment.

4. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. Any disputes arising out of this agreement shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat.
