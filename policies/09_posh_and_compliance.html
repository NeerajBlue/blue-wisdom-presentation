POSH, Anti-Bribery & Compliance

Blue Wisdom maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards unethical conduct, harassment, and corruption.

1. POSH Compliance (Prevention of Sexual Harassment)

The Trainer confirms that they understand and comply with the provisions of the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013 (or equivalent local laws). The Trainer must maintain a safe, respectful, and strictly professional boundary with all participants and client staff.

2. Anti-Bribery & Anti-Corruption

The Trainer shall not offer, promise, give, or authorize any financial or other advantage to any person (including client procurement teams or HR personnel) to secure an improper advantage for themselves or Blue Wisdom.

3. Background Verification (BGV)

By accepting empanelment, the Trainer provides explicit consent for Blue Wisdom to conduct Background Verifications (BGV) if required by an end-client. This may include educational verification, professional reference checks, and criminal record checks.

4. Whistleblower & Escalation

If the Trainer observes any unethical behavior, harassment, or safety violations at the client site, they must immediately escalate the issue to the Blue Wisdom management team before taking independent action.
