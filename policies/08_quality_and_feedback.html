Quality Assurance & Feedback Policy

Blue Wisdom is committed to delivering world-class learning interventions. The quality of our trainers is our biggest asset.

1. Minimum Performance Standards

All trainers are expected to maintain an average participant feedback score of 4.2 out of 5.0 (or equivalent 85% CSAT) across deployments. We monitor both qualitative participant feedback and client stakeholder reviews.

2. Feedback Collection

The Trainer must ensure that Blue Wisdom feedback forms (digital links or physical copies) are filled out by at least 90% of the participants before concluding the final session.

3. Continuous Improvement Protocol

If a Trainer receives a score below 4.0 on a specific module:

  • A mandatory debriefing call will be scheduled with the BW Training Head.
  • The Trainer may be requested to shadow another expert facilitator before being deployed for that specific module again.
  • Consistent poor feedback (two consecutive assignments below par) will trigger an automatic suspension from the active empanelment roster.
