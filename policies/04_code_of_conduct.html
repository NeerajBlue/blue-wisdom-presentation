Trainer Code of Conduct & Professional Standards

Blue Wisdom partners with top-tier organizations. As an empanelled trainer, your conduct reflects directly on our brand reputation.

1. Brand Representation

  • You are acting as a representative of Blue Wisdom Pvt. Ltd. during the entire deployment.
  • Do not distribute personal business cards or promote your independent consultancy to the participants or client stakeholders.
  • If participants ask for contact details, provide your official Blue Wisdom contact or generic LinkedIn profile without pitching services.

2. Punctuality & Readiness

  • Reporting Time: You must report to the training venue at least 45 minutes prior to the scheduled start time.
  • Ensure all AV equipment, presentations, and training materials (workbooks, handouts) are tested and arranged before participants arrive.

3. Dress Code

Business Formal attire is mandatory unless the client specifies a relaxed dress code (e.g., Outbound training). For male trainers: suit, tie, and formal shoes. For female trainers: formal business suits, sarees, or formal kurtas.

4. In-Classroom Behavior

  • Maintain absolute neutrality regarding politics, religion, and controversial social topics.
  • Use inclusive language. Derogatory remarks, sexist jokes, or inappropriate humor are grounds for immediate termination.
  • Respect the client's corporate culture and adhere to their internal facility rules (smoking areas, cafeteria usage).
