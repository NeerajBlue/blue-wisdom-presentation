Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) & Data Privacy

This Non-Disclosure Agreement defines the strict confidentiality obligations of the Trainer while handling materials, participant data, and client information provided by Blue Wisdom.

1. Definition of Confidential Information

Confidential information includes, but is not limited to: participant rosters, contact details, psychometric assessment results, proprietary Blue Wisdom frameworks, presentation slides, client training needs analysis (TNA), and financial terms of the engagement.

2. Obligations of Confidentiality

The Trainer agrees to hold all confidential information in strict confidence and shall not disclose it to any third party without explicit written consent from Blue Wisdom.

3. Data Protection & Participant Privacy

  • Trainers are prohibited from saving participant phone numbers, email addresses, or adding them to personal mailing lists.
  • Trainers may not create unauthorized WhatsApp groups with participants for personal networking.
  • Any photos or videos taken during the workshop must be submitted to Blue Wisdom and cannot be posted online without clearance.

4. Intellectual Property (IP) Protection

All training materials provided by Blue Wisdom remain the exclusive intellectual property of the Company or the end-Client. The Trainer cannot reuse, re-brand, or resell these materials for their own independent consulting work.

5. Survival of Obligations

The obligations of this NDA shall survive the termination of the Trainer's empanelment indefinitely.
