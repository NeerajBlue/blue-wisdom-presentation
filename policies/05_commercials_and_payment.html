Commercials & Payment Policy

Blue Wisdom prides itself on transparent and timely financial transactions with our empanelled experts.

1. Professional Fees

The per-day facilitation fee will be mutually agreed upon in writing (via email or WhatsApp) prior to each specific deployment. This fee is final and inclusive of local commute unless otherwise specified.

2. Invoicing Process

  • Invoices must be submitted through the official Google Form link on the Trainer Hub within 3 working days of completing the assignment.
  • Invoices must be raised on the official Blue Wisdom template or your GST-registered company letterhead.
  • Include your PAN, Bank Account Number, IFSC code, and the specific Project/Client Name.

3. Payment SLA

Blue Wisdom operates on a standard 30-Day Payment SLA. Payments will be processed via NEFT/RTGS within 30 days from the date of the receipt of a hard/soft copy of a correct, error-free invoice and all required post-training documentation (attendance sheets, feedback forms).

4. Statutory Deductions (TDS)

As per Indian Income Tax regulations, Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) will be applicable on all professional fees. Trainers will receive their Form 16A at the end of the financial quarter.

5. Disputed Invoices

If an invoice is incomplete or missing supporting expense receipts, the 30-day SLA will reset on the day the corrected invoice is submitted.
