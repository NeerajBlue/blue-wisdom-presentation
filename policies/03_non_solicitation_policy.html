Non-Solicitation Agreement

This policy protects the business interests and client relationships cultivated by Blue Wisdom Pvt. Ltd.

1. Client Non-Solicitation

The Trainer agrees that during the term of their empanelment, and for a period of twenty-four (24) months immediately following the completion of their last assignment for a specific client through Blue Wisdom, they shall not directly or indirectly:

  • Solicit, approach, or attempt to engage the said client for any independent consulting or training services.
  • Accept any direct contracting offers from the client without routing the business through Blue Wisdom.

2. Employee/Trainer Non-Solicitation

The Trainer shall not recruit, solicit, or induce any other Blue Wisdom empanelled trainer or internal employee to leave their engagement with Blue Wisdom or to join a competing enterprise.

3. Duty to Report

If a client directly approaches the Trainer for future assignments during a Blue Wisdom deployment, the Trainer is professionally obligated to politely redirect the client to the Blue Wisdom account manager.

4. Breach of Policy

A breach of this non-solicitation agreement is a severe violation of professional ethics. Blue Wisdom reserves the right to take legal action, claim damages for lost revenue, and permanently blacklist the Trainer from future empanelment across our network.
