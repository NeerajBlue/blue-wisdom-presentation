Travel & Expense Reimbursement Policy

This policy outlines the guidelines for out-station travel and expense reimbursements for deployed trainers.

1. Flight & Train Bookings

  • All flights must be booked in Economy Class.
  • Train travel should be restricted to 2-Tier AC or 3-Tier AC.
  • Trainers must book tickets at least 14 days in advance to ensure optimal pricing, unless the gig was confirmed last minute.
  • Tickets booked without prior approval of the quoted fare by the BW Account Manager may not be fully reimbursed.

2. Accommodation

Accommodation will be either directly booked by the Client/BW, or reimbursed up to a strict maximum limit. The upper cap is generally ₹3,500 - ₹5,000 per night (inclusive of taxes and breakfast), depending on the city tier. Prior approval of the hotel rate is mandatory.

3. Local Commute & Meals

  • Commute: Reimbursements cover standard Ola/Uber/Airport taxis. Luxury cabs or private driver rentals are not permitted.
  • Meals: When meals are not provided by the hotel or client, a daily per-diem or actual receipt reimbursement limit will apply (typically ₹800 - ₹1,200/day). Alcohol and mini-bar expenses are strictly non-reimbursable.

4. Submission of Receipts

To claim any reimbursement, the trainer must submit the original GST invoices (with the company GSTIN if required) and original Boarding Passes (for flights) through the Trainer Hub Expense Form within 7 days of returning.
