Cancellation & No-Show Policy

Reliability is critical to maintaining client trust. This policy governs cancellations by either the Trainer or the Client.

1. Trainer Cancellation

If a Trainer has accepted a gig but needs to cancel due to an absolute emergency (e.g., severe illness, family emergency), they must notify Blue Wisdom immediately.

  • Less than 72 Hours Notice: Canceling a gig within 72 hours of the start time (without a certified medical emergency) is a severe breach of contract and may result in immediate de-empanelment and liability for client penalties.
  • No-Show: Failing to show up at the venue without prior notice will result in a permanent blacklist, forfeiture of pending invoices, and legal action to recover damages.

2. Client Cancellation

Blue Wisdom strives to protect our trainers' time. If the end-client cancels the assignment:

  • With more than 5 days notice: The gig is rescheduled, and no compensation is paid for the cancellation.
  • Last-Minute Cancellation (Less than 48 hours): Blue Wisdom will advocate on the trainer's behalf to recover a cancellation fee (typically 25% to 50% of the daily fee), subject to the specific client's master service agreement. Non-refundable travel expenses will be fully reimbursed.
